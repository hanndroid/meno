    About

    Welcome to Meno. We're a community of women, men, families and employers who've come together to raise awareness about the menopause and create an environment of support and empowerment for women. We provide education to employers about the menopause so they can understand the needs of their female workforce. We provide women with tools, information, advice and resources to support their health and wellbeing. We provide consultations to GP services so that doctors can support to their female patients in better ways. Our goal is to create a society which is informed and actively supports the billion women globally who are going through menopause right now.

    On this website you'll find a plethora of information and resources including podcasts, guidelines, women's stories, and downloadable infographics that you can complete, download and take to your doctors. We'll also be launching a store that sells products that help menopause symptoms - and only the ones that have been scientifically proven to help relive symptoms. You can rest assured that our product range will be of the highest quality, all with natural, organic, and locally sourced ingredients.
